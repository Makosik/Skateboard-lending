THE NEW GENERATION OF SKATES

When choosing a skateboard, the buyer often faces many questions. And one of the most frequent and most important is the purpose of skating or what kind of board or skateboard do you need?

longboard

Longboard - a type of board for riding at high speeds along mountain serpentines or along tracks. Initially, this type of skating developed even earlier than classic skateboarding, in the 40s and 50s in California. Many surfers switched to asphalt during periods without waves. Now this species has a second life.

cruiser

The cruiser is a kind of “urban longboard”. In contrast to the previously indicated long, cruiser decks are much smaller and therefore easier to maneuver on the streets and in the crowd. This is the most suitable option if the purpose of your riding is a simple ride around the city. As a rule, soft and large wheels are installed on cruiser kits, which easily “swallow” cracks and bumps on the road, and develop a high speed.

plastbord

PENNY board began to develop in Australia and produced a wide variety of plastic skateboards - longs, fishes and cruisers. Their most popular model is considered to be the Original 22″, which is usually what they mean when talking about a penny board.Their main difference is the material. All plastboards are made of plastic and are almost never covered with skin. These are not trick boards, all penny boards are designed exclusively for riding around the city. Very often, this option is recommended for beginners or children who have just begun to master riding on the streets.
